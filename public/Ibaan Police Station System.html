About Us

Ibaan Municipal Police Station is dedicated to maintain peace and order in the Ibaan community. Our team of experienced officers works tirelessly to ensure the safety and security of our residents.

Learn more about our history, mission, and values.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of our community:

  • Emergency response
  • Crime prevention
  • Community outreach programs
  • Investigation and enforcement

Contact us to request our services or report an incident.

Police Officers

Our dedicated police officers are the guardians of justice and order, tirelessly working to ensure safety and security of our community. With unwavering courage and commitment, they stand as protectors, ready to respond to any challenge. Through their dedication and professionalism, they empower our communities, instilling confidence and trust in every interaction. With each officer embodying resilience, resourcefulness, and a commitment to excellence, they lead by example, inspiring others to uphold, the values of integrity and service. Together, they are united in their mission to serve with compassion, to uphold the law, and to make our neighborhoods safer for all.
